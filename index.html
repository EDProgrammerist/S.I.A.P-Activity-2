Suspicious source channels

If you suspected a fake - what to do

  • Don’t forward immediately. Pause and verify first

  • Report to the platform(use the “misinformation” or “impersonation” option).

  • Share a short correction only after reputable fact-checkers confirm it’s fake

  • Verify the source Check the original poster’s profile, credibility, and posting history

  • Preserve evidence: screenshot timestamps, URLs, and usernames(helpful for investigators). Check on fact-checker
